# Urban Store Data Analysis:

## Project Overview:
The **Urban Store Data Analysis** project provides a comprehensive examination of the retail performance and customer behavior of an urban retail business.  
This dataset enables in-depth analysis of **sales performance, customer demographics, order trends, and product popularity**.  

The main objective is to transform raw retail data into actionable insights that help optimize sales strategies, improve customer experience, and forecast future growth.

---

## Dataset Details:

**File Name:** `Urban Store Data Analysis.xlsx`  
**Total Sheets:** 8  

| Sheet Name | Description | Rows | Columns |
|-------------|--------------|-------|----------|
| **Urban Store Report** | Dashboard or high-level summary of the store’s annual performance | 0 | 1 |
| **Urban Store** | Main dataset containing customer, order, and sales data | 31,047 | 21 |
| **Orders Channel** | Breakdown of sales performance across different order channels (online, offline, etc.) | 9 | 2 |
| **Age Vs Gender** | Comparison of purchase trends between genders and age groups | 6 | 3 |
| **Men Vs Women** | Summary of male vs female customer contributions to total sales | 4 | 2 |
| **Order Status** | Overview of order outcomes – completed, returned, canceled, etc. | 6 | 2 |
| **Top 5** | List of top-performing categories, items, or regions | 7 | 2 |
| **Sales Vs Order** | Monthly analysis showing relationship between total sales and number of orders | 14 | 3 |

---

## Columns in Main Dataset (Urban Store):

Key columns in the main sheet include:

- **Order ID:** Unique identifier for each transaction  
- **Cust ID:** Customer identifier  
- **Gender:** Customer gender (Male/Female)  
- **Age / Age Group:** Age and corresponding category  
- **Date / Month:** Order date and month of purchase  
- **Status:** Order completion status (Delivered, Returned, Cancelled)  
- **Channel:** Platform used for purchase (Online, In-store, etc.)  
- **Sales Amount / Quantity:** Represents revenue and quantity sold  

---

## Objectives of the Analysis:

- Identify **most profitable customer segments** based on age and gender  
- Analyze **sales performance across channels** (online vs offline)  
- Examine **order status distribution** to understand fulfillment efficiency  
- Highlight **top-performing products or categories**  
- Explore **monthly sales trends** and correlation between number of orders and revenue  
- Support **data-driven decision-making** through dashboards and summaries  

---

## Key Insights (Expected Outcomes):

- **Sales Growth Trends:** Identify peak months and seasonal variations  
- **Customer Demographics:** Discover which age group and gender shop most frequently  
- **Channel Analysis:** Determine which platforms drive the most revenue  
- **Order Fulfillment:** Assess efficiency by analyzing order status distribution  
- **Top Performers:** Highlight top 5 categories or items contributing to the majority of revenue
